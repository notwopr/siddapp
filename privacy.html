Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2025

Sidd is a communication aid designed to help autistic and nonverbal individuals express themselves. We respect your privacy and do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data without your consent.

Data Collection

Sidd does not collect any user-identifiable data. All user interactions, including communication logs, are stored locally on the user's device.

Location Data

If enabled, Sidd may access your location only when you press a communication button to locally log the time and location of that action. This data is not transmitted or shared.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party libraries to enable core functionality. These services do not collect user data.
